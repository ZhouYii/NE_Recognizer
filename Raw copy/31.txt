NEWTOWN, Conn.  A spokesman for the family of Adam Lanza, the gunman in the school massacre here, identified Mr. Lanzas father on Monday as the person who claimed his body.

The father, Peter Lanza, received his 20-year-old sons remains at the Connecticut chief medical examiners office.

The spokesman, Errol Cockfield Jr., said private arrangements occurred during the weekend, but he gave no details.

The younger Mr. Lanza killed his mother at the home they shared, then he killed 20 children and six adult staff members at the Sandy Hook Elementary School on the morning of Dec. 14, in one of the deadliest such shootings in the nations history. He later killed himself as the police arrived at the school.

The police have not offered a motive for the rampage, and the investigation continues.
